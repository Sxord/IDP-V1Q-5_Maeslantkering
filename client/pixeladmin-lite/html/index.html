Maeslantkering

Our mission

The Maeslantkering protects the harbor of Rotterdam against the North Sea. When the sea level rises the harbor is at danger. If the water gets too high, the Maeslantkering must close. However, the failure probability is too high. To reduce the chance of failure without making the Maeslantkering more unsafe, a Proof-of-Concept must come. Our team is tasked with this project. Our mission is to create a working scale replica that mimics the functionality of the Maeslantkering.

Build a Proof-of-Concept for the Maeslantkering and present the demo in 3 weeks.

Brian van der Bijl as Teacher Hogeschool Utrecht
